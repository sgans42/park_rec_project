Bowling Green Parks and Recreation

Explore the beauty of our parks and recreation facilities!

Mission

Our City Parks and Rec is dedicated to providing high-quality parks, facilities, and programs to enhance the quality of life for all residents and visitors.

Parks!

  • Fountain Square Park
  • Ogden Park
  • Covington Woods Park
  • White Parks Inc
  • Basil Griffin Park
  • Hobson Grove Park
  • ...and so many more!

The extensive list of Bowling Green parks host a range of activities such as disk golf, basketball, children's playgrounds, and outdoor gyms!

Recreational Centers!

  • F.O. Moxley Community Center ($3/yr Membership)
  • Parker-Bennett Community Center
  • Kummer/Little Recreational Center ($3/yr Membership)

For a VERY low annual membership you can enjoy indoor basketball courts, pools, ping-pong tables, billiards, and much more!

Attractions!

  • Beech Bend Waterpark & Rides
  • Russell Sims Waterpark
  • Corvette Museum
  • Civil War Discovery Trail
  • Railroad Museum
  • Aviation Heritage Park

All of our attractions are rich in history and fun! Check these out if you are in Bowling Green, KY!
